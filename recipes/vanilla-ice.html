Lasagna

This is a recipe for tasty lasagna. Not vegeterian or vegan recipe

Ingredients

  • milk
  • vanilla
  • ice
  • salt

  1. Mix milk and vanilla
  2. Put ice in a bowl
  3. On top of the ice bowl set the bowl with vanilla and milk
  4. Get some salt on the ice
  5. Put everything in the freezer
