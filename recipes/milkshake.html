Extremly good milkshake. The best recipe you will find

Ingredients

  • milk
  • banana
  • ice cream
  • strawberry

  1. Peel banana and cut the green part of the strawberry
  2. Cut both into small pieces
  3. Mix fruits and milk in a blender
  4. Now add the ice cream and stirr
  5. Use 1 strawberry for topping
